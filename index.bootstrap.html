We love to support higher education.

The Contra Costa Association of Realtors Scholarship Foundation provides college scholarship grants to qualifying students in the fields of finance, business and real estate. Our commitment to support higher education is the heart of the Contra Costa Association of Realtors' desire to make a difference!
