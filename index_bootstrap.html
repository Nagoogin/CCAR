We love to support higher education.

Title

The Contra Costa Association of Realtors® (CCAR) Scholarship Foundation provides college scholarship grants to qualifying students in the fields of finance, business and real estate. Our commitment to support higher education is the heart of the Contra Costa Association of Realtors®' desire to make a difference! In the last five years alone, the Foundation has awarded nearly $250,000 in college grants to deserving students whose families reside in central Contra Costa County.
